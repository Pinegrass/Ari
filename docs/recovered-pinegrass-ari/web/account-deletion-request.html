Account deletion

Delete your Ari account

You can request deletion of your Ari account and all associated data here. This page is provided so you can delete your account without installing the app.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and login
  • All transactions and budgets you have logged
  • All devices linked to your account

What we keep

Anonymized, aggregated records required by law (e.g. payment audit trails). Nothing that can identify you. We retain these for up to 90 days before purging.

How long it takes

Within 30 days of your request, your data will be fully purged from our systems. You will receive an email confirmation when the deletion is complete.

We will email you a confirmation link. Click the link to confirm — this prevents accidental deletions. If you have the app installed, you can also delete your account directly from Settings → Delete account without using this form.

Questions? Email support@pinegrass.app. This page is provided to comply with Google Play and App Store account-deletion requirements.
